His name is Ziggy.

Ziggy was a kitten when my mother rescued him from a hoarder's house. He was such an adorable little cat. He, like most cats, was also a bit of a jerk, but I loved him all the same. He used to climb on my shoulder when he was a kitten. He has really mellowed out since his days as a kitten; which makes glad because--boy-- he was a wild one.

Here are a list of things he enjoys:

  • Sleeping
  • Eating
  • Running around at 2AM
  • Treats
  • Standing in front of the heater
